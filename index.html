About Myself

As a caregiver, I witnessed firsthand the financial challenges faced by individuals and families dealing with medical crises. It was heart-wrenching to see people struggling to access life-saving treatments or necessary medical procedures due to financial constraints. Witnessing the impact that financial burdens had on their well-being inspired me to take action and find a solution.
